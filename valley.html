Overview

Stardew Valley contains a wide range of locations, from mountains to a beach to the most important place, your farm. In this section, we will go over most of the buildings and locations within the Valley.

Pelican Town

Pelican Town is the town where the Player moves to at the start of the game, located in Stardew Valley. Pelican Town is where most of the villagers live, work, and socialize.

The northwest entrance to town connects to the Bus Stop, and The Farm. The southwest entrance connects to Cindersap Forest, which contains Marnie's Ranch, Leah's Cottage, and the Wizard's Tower. Directly south of town is The Beach. North of town connects to The Moutain, with the Carpenter's Shop, The Mines, Adventurer's Guild, the Railrood, and the Quarry.

Pierre's General Store

Pierre's General Store is where you can buy various seeds, saplings, fertilizer, and other useful items. After you have 8 friendship hearts with a marrigeable villager, a bouquet of flowers will be available for purchase. You can also sell foraged items, crops, artisan goods, and animal products.

Some products in stock will vary from season to season, such as seeds.

At first, Pierre's General Store is closed on Wednesday. However, if you finish all of the Community Center bundles, Pierre's General Store will be open 7 days a week.

You can enter the building until 9:00PM, but Pierre leaves the sales counter at 5:00PM every day, so you cannot purcahse or sell items after 5:00PM.

Clinc

Harvey's Clinic is located in the center of Pelican Town, next to Pierre's General Store. Here you can buy Energy Tonic and Muscle Remedy, which restore Health and Stamina.

Although you can enter the Clinic most says between 9:00AM and 3:00PM, you cannot purchase medical supplies unless Maru or Harvey are behind the supplies counter.

Community Center

The Community Center is located on the screen north of Pierre's General Store.

To activate the Community Center Bundles, you must trigger a cut scene with Lewis. Enter Pelican Town from the Bus Stop screen on a sunny day from Spring 5th onward on between 8:00AM and 1:00PM. If you enter town from another direction, time, or on a day that is not sunny, the cut scene will not trigger.

In the cut scene, Lewis leads you to the Community Center, where you will find creatures called Junimos that fade in and out of existence, along with a glowing placard covered in strange writing. Once you attempt to read the placard, you will receive a letter from the Wizard on the day after and be able to enter his house. Going there triggers a cut scene in which the Wizard talks to you about the Junimos. Consuming the Wizard's potion activates the bundles. You can then return to the Community Center to read the placards, understand them, and give the item bundle offerings to the Junimos.

Alternatively, the Community Center can become the Joja Warehouse if the Player chooses to purchase a membership from Morris at JojaMart. If the Player does this, the Warehouse building will replace the Community Center and all unlocks are simply purchased with gold instead of item bundles.

Museum

The Museum can be found in the south-east corner of Pelican Town, south of the Blacksmith.

The Museum consists of a library with an attached gallery for displaying the Museum's collection. At the start of the game, the entire collection is empty. The Player may donate artifacts and books dug up from their farm or the mines in exchange for rewards.

Penny holds lessons for the towns children, Vincent and Jas, within the library on Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Fridays.

Blacksmith

The Blacksmith serves as a location for purchasing coal and ores (copper, iron, and gold), upgrading tools, and breaking open geodes. It also is the home of blacksmith Clint.

JojaMart

Buying a Membership at JojaMart from Morris for 5,000 gold causes the Community Center to be destroyed and replaced with a Joja Warehouse. The Player can then buy the town refubishments through JojaMart, rather than through competing item bundles in the Community Center. It should be noted that this alternate branch in gameplay encourages a very different, money-driven playstyle than the exploration one offered through improving the Community Center; rather than requiring a variety of items from different seasons to complete item bundles, large sums of gold are paid upfront for improvements.

JojaMart offers brand products at a relatively cheap price, but sells seeds at ludicrous prices.

The Stardrop Saloon

The Stardrop Saloon is owned by Gus, and is located in the center of Pelican Town. It's a meeting place for many villagers, and hosts a variety of entertainment,including fully-playable arcade machines, a jukebox, and a Joja Cola soda machine.

The Beach

The Beach is located to the south of Pelican Town. During the Summer the Luau and Dance of the Moonlight Jellies festivals are held on the beach.

[click to expand]

Elliott's Cabin

Elliott's Cabin is the home of Elliott. It's located directly east when entering the Beach. You cannot enter without first gaining at least two friendship hearts with Elliott.

Fish Shop

The Fish Shop is the home of Willy. It is located on the docks of the Beach, south of Pelican Town.

Willy offers a variety of fishing supplies inside, including rods, bait, tackle, and crab pots. More goods become available as the Player increases their fishing skill by catching fish, with the Iridium Rod being sold as soon as level 6 fishing skill is obtained, without waiting for the next day.

You can also sell fish or shells to Willy during the daytime when he's behind his counter.

Cindersap Forest

Cindersap Forest is the area located south of the Player's Farm, and includes the Abandoned House, Leah's Cottage, and Marnie's Ranch. It has exits to the north into the Farm, the east into Pelican Town, south into the sewers, and to the nortwest into the Secret Woods. As expected, Cindersap Forest contains a large variety of trees. These trees behave normally, yet do not need to be re-planted once they have been chopped down. During the start of spring, you can find wild Spring Onions in the dirt spots to the southeast of the map, near the sewers.

Marnie's Ranch

Marnie's Ranch is located in the northeast area of Cindersap Forest, near the southwest entrance to Pelican Town. The Ranch is occupied by Marnie, along with her niece Jas, and nephew Shane.

Marnie runs her shop from 9:00AM to 4:00PM each day, except Monday and Tuesday. Here you can buy hay for your animals, heaters, milk pails, and shears. Marnie's Ranch is also where you buy your livestock.

Leah's Cottage

Leah's Cottage is the home of Leah. It is located south of the Player's Farm and Marnie's Ranch in the Cindersap Forest area. You cannot enter without first gaining at least two friendship hearts with Leah.

Abandoned House

The Abandoned House is located in the south of Cindersap Forest. A mouse takes over the building and opens a shop there selling hats to the player after the first achievement is acquired. All hats are sold for 1,000 gold. Hats are purely cosmetic. A new hat will appear in the shop whenever an achievement gets unlocked.

Secret Woods

The Secret Woods is an area that can be entered from the northwest corner of Cindersap Forest, south of the Player's Farm. The entrance is initially blocked by a fallen log that can only be cleanred after upgrading to a Steel Axe.

The Secret Woods is full of Slimes that will attack the player.

The Secret Woods contains six stumps that regrow daily that can be harvest for a total of 12 hardwood each day.

The Mountain

The Mountain is the area located north of Pelican Town. To the west is an exit to the Backwoods, a path that runs from the north of the Player's Farm to the west of the Mountains. Through a pass to the north lies a connecion to the Railroad.

Carpenter's Shop

Robin's Carpenter Shop is located in the west area of the Mountain. This is where Robin runs her carpentry business, and where her husband Demetrius and children Maru and Sebatian reside.

Here is where you can purchase all farm buildings and farm building upgrades, including for the Player's Farmhouse. You can also purchase furniture.

Robin will begin construction the day after buildings are purchased, but she does not work on festival days. Most construction projects are finished in 2 days.

Linus's Tent

Linus's Tent is the home of Linus. It is located northeast of Robin's Carpenter Shop.

The Mines

The Mines are located to the north. The Mines are monster-infested, but also the primary source for stone and ores. There are 120 floors to the Mines, with monster difficulty increasing as the Player descends.

Adventurer's Guild

The Adventurer's Guild is located to the right from the entrance to the Mines. Marlon and Gil reside here. Once access is gained, it provides a shop selling weapons, boots, and rings. The Player can also purchase monster parts.

The Railroad

The Railroad is located to the north of the Mountain. It becomes accessible after an earthquake occurs on the 3rd of Summer in the first year.

On random days between 9:00AM and 6:00PM, a train can pass through the area. If the Player is outdoors, a message will appear in the lower right portion of the screen saying "A train is passing through Stardew Valley" accompanied by a train whistle.

When the train cars pass, items such as Stone, Coal, Iron Ore, Wood, and Geodes may fall off and be picked up. This requires the Player to be present as the train passes.

The Spa

In the southwest portion of the Railroad is the Spa. Standing still when in the pool causes energy and health to be restored.
